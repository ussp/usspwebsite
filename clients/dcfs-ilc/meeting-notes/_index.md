# Meeting Notes Index — DCFS/ILC AI Rollout

> **How to use this folder:**
> 1. Drop raw transcripts into `_inbox/` (any filename, any format).
> 2. They get sorted into `status/raw/` (Krasan-internal recurring meetings) or `team/raw/` (DCFS team working sessions) with dated filenames.
> 3. An extracted summary is written next to them (same name, `-summary.md` suffix).
> 4. Findings flow into `../planning/rollout-plan.md`, `../planning/deliverables/assumptions-and-risks.md`, and `../planning/daily-calendar.md`.
> 5. A one-line entry is appended to this index below.
