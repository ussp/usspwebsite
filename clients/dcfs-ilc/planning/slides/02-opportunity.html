Illinois Connect — The Opportunity

The Program

Illinois Connect is a SACWIS to CCWIS migration — modernizing the State's child welfare system on Microsoft Dynamics 365. Across 12 SAFe product teams and 160+ Krasan consultants, this ART is delivering critical capabilities for DCFS.

Why AI, Why Now

  • AI is becoming standard for high-performing delivery teams
  • The State has already invested in GitHub Copilot and Atlassian Rovo
  • Teams are delivering well — AI builds on a strong foundation
  • Krasan is developing a structured AI transformation framework — designed for engagements like ILC
Objective: Apply AI to SAFe delivery processes — pilot with select teams, measure the impact, scale what works.

Our Approach
