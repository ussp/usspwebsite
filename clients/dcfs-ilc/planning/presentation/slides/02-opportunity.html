Illinois Connect — The Opportunity

The Program

Illinois Connect is migrating three legacy systems — SACWIS, CYCIS, and MARS — to a unified CCWIS platform on Microsoft Dynamics 365. Across 12 SAFe product teams and 160+ Krasan consultants, this ART is delivering critical capabilities for DCFS.

Why AI, Why Now

  • AI is becoming standard for high-performing delivery teams
  • The State has already invested in GitHub Copilot and Atlassian Rovo
  • Teams are delivering well — AI builds on a strong foundation
  • Krasan is developing a structured AI transformation framework — designed for engagements like ILC
Target: 10-15% productivity improvement within 13 weeks (1 PI) — measured by tangible outputs, not story points.

Our Approach

Amplify, Don't Replace

  • Same people, better tools
  • No workforce reduction
  • AI assists, human decides

Measure, Don't Guess

  • Baseline first, then compare
  • Tangible outputs: LOC, docs, config
  • Before/after with control group

Pilot, Then Scale

  • Start with 2 teams (Krasan roles)
  • AI adoption from day 1 under HITL
  • Scale what works
