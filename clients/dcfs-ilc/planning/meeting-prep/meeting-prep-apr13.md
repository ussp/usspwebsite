## What to Listen For

| If Jim says... | It means... | Action |
|----------------|-------------|--------|
| "I want numbers" | Quantitative KPIs matter most | Prioritize velocity, cycle time, defect rate |
| "I want efficiency" | Focus on time-saving processes | Story writing, test generation tracks |
| "I've done AI before" | He's not a novice — don't over-explain | Reference his NLP work, build on it |
| "I'm worried about compliance" | Front-load governance | Make compliance map a pre-pilot deliverable |
| "Just show me it works" | Compress setup, get to results fast | Shorten pre-pilot, get to measurable pilot quickly |
| "What tools can we use?" | Tool access is uncertain | Critical to resolve before planning training |

## Do NOT

- Over-present — this is a listening session
- Promise specific improvement percentages
- Say we're "changing" processes — we're augmenting them
- Present the framework as already proven — it's our intent, what we're building
- Use "use cases" — say "SDLC processes"
