## Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use AI as a starting point, then refine | Don't submit AI output without review |
| Feed AI well-structured input | Don't feed poorly written stories — AI makes them worse |
| Verify AI suggestions against domain knowledge | Don't assume AI understands child welfare business rules |
| Use Rovo (has Confluence context) over generic Copilot for stories | Don't use tools outside state-approved list |
