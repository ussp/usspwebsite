## What They Do

Per4m.AI helps enterprises close the **AI Execution Gap** — the disconnect between AI ambition and organizational readiness. They combine AI-driven assessment with strategic consulting to prepare workforces and organizational structures for AI adoption.

## Core Service Pillars

### 1. SYNTHESIZE — Organizational Assessment
- Provides "100% organizational visibility" via proprietary platform
- Replaces traditional sampling with comprehensive data collection
- Analyzes teams and individuals simultaneously

### 2. ARCHITECT — Framework Design
- Combines human talent strategy with agentic AI ecosystems
- Quantified risk assessment and maturity modeling
- Structural blueprints for AI-ready organizations

### 3. ACCELERATE — Implementation Enablement
- Customized training paths based on identified skill gaps
- AI-monitored growth and performance loops
- Measurable upskilling outcomes

## Target Market

- Fortune 1000 enterprises
- Organizations struggling to realize AI ROI due to lacking structural support
- Companies seeking to quantify AI readiness

- USSP spent 2 years securing the TOPS contract and the Krasan relationship — Per4m.AI does not get direct access to the client or contract

### Preferred Structure Options (in order of preference)

**Option A: Advisory/Methodology License (preferred)**
- Josh serves as an **advisor to USSP**
- USSP pays a flat fee or retainer for access to his methodology (SYNTHESIZE/ARCHITECT/ACCELERATE framework)
- USSP delivers the work to the client using his methodology — Josh never touches the client
- USSP owns the client relationship, deliverables, and execution entirely
- Cleanest structure: no subcontractor paperwork, no state vendor registration needed for Per4m.AI
- Josh gets recurring revenue without needing government contracting infrastructure
- USSP builds internal capability over time using his framework

**Option B: Subcontractor under USSP/Krasan**
- Per4m.AI as a sub to USSP for assessment/tooling
- USSP marks up his rate or takes percentage of platform fees
- All client communication goes through USSP/Krasan — Per4m.AI behind the scenes
- More complex: requires sub agreements, possibly state vendor registration
