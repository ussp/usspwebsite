## Contract Process

1. Sign "Subcontract Enrollment Contract" with Krasan
   - Includes: Subcontractor Services Agreement + Requisition Process
2. Submit mandatory documents:
   - Completed W-9 Form
   - Current BEP (Business Enterprise Program) Certification Letter
   - Certificate of Good Standing from state of incorporation
   - Certificate of Insurance naming Krasan Consulting Services, LLC AND the State of Illinois as additionally insured
   - Voided Check or Bank Letter for ACH Payment Processing

## Consultant Submission Requirements

When submitting a consultant through Krasan:

1. Submit consultant in **Oorwin** (Krasan's ATS)
2. Provide updated **resume**
3. Provide signed **Right to Represent (RTR)**
4. Provide valid **identity proof (ID)**
5. Provide **employment authorization** (if required)

---

## Onboarding Requirements

When a consultant is being onboarded for a client through Krasan:

1. Valid **identity proof (ID)**
2. **Employment authorization** (if required)
3. **Address proof** (utility bill to confirm US residency)
4. **Signed forms** (if required by specific client)
