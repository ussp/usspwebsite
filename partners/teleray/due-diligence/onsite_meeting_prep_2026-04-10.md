## PART 1: QUESTIONS FOR THE CEO

### A. Virtual Nursing — The Core Thesis

1. You believe virtual nursing is the big market. **Walk me through the TAM as you see it** — how many hospitals, what spend per bed, what's the realistic addressable market in 3-5 years?
2. You have **zero virtual nursing deployments today**. What is the pipeline? Signed LOIs? Active pilots? Hospitals in contract negotiation?
3. **What is the timeline** to first live virtual nursing site generating revenue?
4. Your 3,000+ radiology sites are the cross-sell base. **How many have expressed interest** in virtual nursing? Is there a defined sales motion, or is this aspirational?
5. **Who is the buyer** for virtual nursing — CNO, CFO, CIO? Is it the same buyer as your radiology product? If not, do you have relationships with the virtual nursing buyer?
6. Care.ai has 1,500+ sites and Stryker distribution. **Have you been in any competitive evaluations against Care.ai?** What happened?
7. Your catalog leads with Axis cameras and security. Saladi (Optum) had never heard of Axis. **Are you pitching the right message** to CNOs and CFOs, or are you leading with tech that the buyer doesn't care about?

### B. Epic Integration — The Linchpin

8. Is TeleRay listed on **Epic App Orchard / Marketplace** (formally validated), or is this a custom HL7/FHIR connection?
9. Is the **billing integration specifically validated by Epic**? Epic applies extra scrutiny to billing — this is the moat if validated.
10. Does the integration **auto-populate the encounter note AND attach CPT/billing codes**, or does a human coder still intervene?
11. **How many sites are actively billing** through the Epic integration today — not contracted, not piloted, but submitting claims?
12. Can you **demo the billing workflow live** — from virtual encounter to Epic encounter note to claim generation?
13. What is the integration depth with **Cerner/Oracle Health and Meditech**? Small/rural hospitals — your strongest market — often run these, not Epic.

### C. Small/Rural Hospital Strategy

14. Saladi's strongest insight: small hospitals lose patients (and revenue) every time they transfer. **Is this your primary go-to-market**, or are you also chasing large health systems?
15. How does a **rural hospital without Epic** use your platform? What's the integration story for Meditech, CPSI, Athena?
16. What does a **typical rural deployment look like** — number of cameras, rooms, cost to the hospital, monthly recurring?
17. Are you working with **Rural Health Clinics (RHC) or Critical Access Hospitals (CAH)**? They have different reimbursement rules that could accelerate adoption.

### D. Financials & Business Model

18. **Current ARR** and growth rate?
19. Revenue breakdown: **what percentage** is SaaS recurring vs. hardware vs. professional services?
20. **Gross margins** on each revenue stream?
21. **Churn rate** on the radiology installed base?
22. **Average contract value** — radiology vs. virtual care?
23. **Burn rate and runway** — how long until you need additional capital?
24. What are you **raising and at what valuation**? What does the use of funds look like?

### E. Team & Execution

25. **Actual headcount** — engineering, sales, support, leadership?
26. What **key hires** are planned in the next 12 months?
27. Who on the team has **hospital sales experience** selling to CNOs/CFOs (not just radiology departments)?
28. What is the **engineering roadmap** for the next 12 months — what ships when?

### F. Regulatory & Risk

29. Is TeleRay working with **CMS or AMA on a dedicated virtual nursing CPT code**?
30. Current telehealth flexibilities expire **December 31, 2027**. What is your contingency if they're not extended?
31. What is your **HIPAA incident history**? Any breaches, complaints, or OCR investigations?
32. What is the **IP portfolio** — the catalog mentions "multiple patents pending." What's the status?

## PART 2: WHAT TO OBSERVE & GATHER ON-SITE

### Product Demo (REQUEST THESE)

- [ ] **Live Epic billing workflow** — virtual encounter to encounter note to CPT code generation. This is the #1 claim to validate.
- [ ] **Live image exchange** — send a DICOM study between two endpoints. Watch the P2P encryption in action.
- [ ] **Virtual nursing station** — see the nurse-side interface. How many rooms can one nurse monitor? How do alerts work?
- [ ] **AI alerts demo** — fall detection, cough detection, aggression. Are these production-ready or prototype?
- [ ] **TeleRay.Live** — watch a live modality streaming session. How is latency? Frame rate?
- [ ] **Reporting dashboard** — what analytics does the hospital admin see?

### Physical Office Observations

- [ ] **Team size reality check** — how many people are actually in the office? Does it match the headcount they claim?
- [ ] **Engineering team** — ask to meet 2-3 engineers. Gauge depth. Are they building or maintaining?

- [ ] **Support operation** — where is support handled? US-based as claimed? How many support staff?
- [ ] **Hardware inventory** — are carts, cameras, RMC units in stock? Manufacturing partner? Lead times?

## PART 3: RED FLAGS TO WATCH FOR

| Red Flag | What It Means |
|----------|---------------|
| CEO won't demo the Epic billing workflow live | The auto-billing claim may be theoretical |
| No LOIs or signed pilots for virtual nursing | Pre-product, not pre-revenue — higher risk |
| Team is < 15 people | Can't execute on radiology + virtual nursing + hardware simultaneously |
| No one on the team has sold to CNOs/CFOs | They'll be selling to the wrong buyer |
| Can't name 3 reference customers willing to talk | Customer satisfaction may not match the testimonials |
| Pipeline tool is a spreadsheet | Sales process is immature |
| Avoids financial questions | Cash position may be worse than presented |
| All revenue is hardware/services, minimal SaaS | Not a recurring revenue business, harder to scale |
| Patents are only "pending" with no granted | IP moat is weaker than implied |
| No SOC 2 or HITRUST | Will be blocked by large hospital procurement |
