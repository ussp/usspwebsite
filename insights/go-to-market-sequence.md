# USSP Go-to-Market Sequence: IL State Government

**Date:** March 2026
**Core Insight:** Lead with TeleRay + Axis. Edge AI comes second.

---

## Why This Order

| Factor | TeleRay + Axis | VionOS.ai (Edge AI) |
|---|---|---|
| Deployments | 3,000 sites (radiology) | Zero US deployments |
| Hardware partner | Axis (Canon-owned, global brand) | ATRI (unknown in US) |
| Market proof | Prison telehealth proven ($780M saved in TX) | No correctional deployments anywhere |
| Liability | Low — telehealth supplements doctor visits | High — safety monitoring in juvenile facilities |
| Dinkar's reaction | Not yet pitched | Already pushed back |
| Revenue clarity | $150/bed/month, simple math | Unproven, per-camera pricing untested |
| Time to revenue | Weeks (proven product, resell + implement) | Months (need proof deployments first) |

---

## Phase 1: TeleRay + Axis → Dinkar → State Agencies

**Goal:** Land the first TOPS task order for telehealth in an IL state facility.

**Pitch to Dinkar:**
- "Proven telehealth platform, 3,000 sites, Axis privacy cameras, BIPA compliant, per-bed pricing. We resell and implement."
- Low risk for Krasan — the product already works, the market is proven
- Revenue is clear — $150/bed/month × number of beds × number of facilities
- MBE/WBE certification helps agencies meet diversity goals

**Target facilities (in order of approachability):**
1. **IDOC (Corrections)** — 25+ facilities with healthcare units. Telehealth reduces inmate transport ($350/visit). Specialist access without security risk. This is the strongest use case.
2. **IL Veterans homes** — 4 state-run homes. Aging population, staffing shortages, need virtual nursing and specialist consults.
3. **IDHS developmental centers** — mental health facilities, remote specialist access.
4. **IDJJ (Juvenile Justice)** — youth facility healthcare. Smaller scale but relevant.

**What USSP delivers:**
- Resell TeleRay subscription (margin: $70-80/bed/month)
- Deploy and configure the platform
- Integrate with facility EMR/EHR systems
- Install/configure Axis cameras if needed
- Train staff
- Ongoing support and managed services

**Success criteria for Phase 1:**
- Dinkar agrees to present TeleRay to at least one state agency
- First TOPS task order issued
- First facility deployed and running

---

## Phase 2: VionOS.ai (Edge AI) → Add-On to Existing Relationships

**Goal:** Once USSP is inside a facility with TeleRay, introduce facility safety monitoring.

**Timing:** After Phase 1 has at least one successful deployment AND childcare/Launch proof deployments have data (see edge-ai-deployment-plan.md).

**The pitch (to the same facility):**
- "While we're deploying telehealth cameras, we can also add facility safety monitoring on the same infrastructure"
- Staff-to-youth ratio monitoring, zone occupancy, injury detection
- Uses existing camera infrastructure (potentially same Axis cameras)
- Different buyer within the same facility (operations/safety vs. healthcare)

**Why this works:**
- You're already a trusted vendor inside the facility
- You've already proven you can deliver (TeleRay is running)
- Dinkar has seen USSP execute — he's more likely to back the Edge AI pitch now
- The facility already has cameras deployed — VionOS.ai is just adding an analytics layer

**The pitch to Dinkar (Phase 2):**
- "We deployed TeleRay at Facility X. It's running, they're happy. Now the operations side wants facility safety monitoring. We have proof from our childcare and Launch deployments. Here are the numbers."
- Completely different conversation than pitching Edge AI cold with no proof

---

## Phase 3: Combined Offering

**Goal:** Position USSP as the single vendor for clinical intelligence + facility intelligence in IL state facilities.

**What USSP offers:**
- TeleRay — telehealth, virtual nursing, imaging (clinical)
- Axis cameras — privacy-compliant hardware with AI masking (infrastructure)
- VionOS.ai — facility safety, staff ratios, zone monitoring, injury detection (operations)
- All through one MBE/WBE certified subvendor via TOPS

**This is the long-term differentiation.** No large integrator is offering this combination. No single technology partner covers all three. USSP is the glue.

---

## Parallel Track: Build Edge AI Proof (Don't Wait)

While pursuing TeleRay with Dinkar, continue the Edge AI proof deployments in parallel:

1. Deploy at childcare center (staff ratios — closest to IDJJ use case)
2. Deploy at Launch (40 cameras, injury detection, scale)
3. Collect 60-90 days of data
4. Package results

This runs independently of the TeleRay sales cycle. When Phase 1 lands and you're ready for Phase 2, the proof is already there.

---

## Key Principle

TeleRay gets you in the door. Edge AI expands the relationship. Don't lead with the thing Dinkar already pushed back on. Lead with the thing that's proven, low-risk, and has clear revenue — then earn the right to pitch the harder sell once you're a trusted vendor inside the facility.
