# Edge AI — Krasan CEO Feedback

**Source:** Dinkar, CEO of Krasan Consulting Services (TOPS Prime Partner)
**Date:** March 2026
**Product:** VionOS.ai / Edge AI for Facility Safety

---

## Feedback 1: Large Implementors Own This Space

**What Dinkar said:** Edge AI / computer vision projects are typically done by large system integrators. It's a hard sell for a smaller firm.

**Why it matters:**
- Agencies default to big names (Accenture, Deloitte, CGI) for infrastructure-heavy, "new technology" projects
- A smaller firm pitching the same thing head-to-head struggles to win on credibility alone
- As the TOPS prime, Krasan needs to feel confident in what they're subcontracting — if the project fails, it reflects on them too

---

## Feedback 2: No Proof of Deployment in High-Liability Environments

**What Dinkar said:** These are high-risk environments (corrections, juvenile justice, healthcare). There's a lot of liability. Buyers need proof that this has been done before.

**Why it matters:**
- Procurement officers in correctional/healthcare facilities won't take a chance on unproven technology — their career is on the line
- A capability brief with no case study is not enough for high-liability buyers
- Krasan as the prime shares the liability risk — if it fails, it reflects on Krasan too
- No amount of marketing copy overcomes the trust gap without real-world proof

---

## Critical Context: Government Access

- USSP does not have direct IL government access for TOPS
- USSP is a subvendor under Krasan (TOPS prime)
- Dinkar controls whether USSP gets presented to state agencies
- Any offering (Edge AI, TeleRay, or combined) must first convince Dinkar before it reaches a state buyer
- Without Krasan buy-in, USSP would need another TOPS prime or go direct (full RFP process)

## Response: Deployment Plan

These two pieces of feedback led directly to the phased deployment plan (see `edge-ai-deployment-plan.md`):
1. Deploy at investor-owned childcare center (staff ratios — same use case as IDJJ)
2. Deploy at Launch entertainment center (40 cameras, injury detection, scale)
3. Package evidence with real data
4. Present to Dinkar with proof — TeleRay + Axis + VionOS.ai as a complete package
5. If Dinkar agrees, Krasan presents to state agency via TOPS
