What Is TeleRay?

Cloud-based healthcare platform — Austin, TX / Chicago, IL

Virtual Care

Multi-room video consultations, virtual nursing, telesitting, ICU monitoring. Browser-based — no app downloads for providers or patients.

Cloud PACS / Imaging

DICOM image storage and exchange, FDA-approved viewer, legacy study migration. This is where their current revenue comes from (3,000+ sites).

Clinical Tools

TeleRay Reporting (customizable), TeleRay Record (HD surgical/endoscopy video to DICOM), TeleRay Remote (remote modality control).

Integration

250+ EMR/EHR integrations (Epic, Cerner, Athena). Built on Microsoft Azure. HIPAA compliant. Implementations typically completed within two weeks.

Recommended Approach

Reseller first, investment in parallel

Step 1: Sign reseller agreement with IL government exclusivity now. Start selling immediately.

Step 2: Run investment due diligence in parallel (financials, cap table, churn, Axis status).

Step 3: Close investment once we have real sales data and completed due diligence.

This is fair for both sides: TeleRay gets a channel partner selling into IL government today. USSP validates the opportunity before committing $500K+. Early sales strengthen both the partnership and the investment case.
