# Payment Milestones, PMU & Recurring Costs

**Source:** Letter VTS Guidelines, Sections 7, 8, 10, 11

## Payment Milestones

| # | Deliverable | Payment | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Signing of MoU (MoRTH & State/UT) | **80%** | T + 15 days |
| 2 | Commissioning of Monitoring Centre by State/UT | — | T + 180 days (latest 31 Dec 2020) |
| 3 | 3 months of successful MC operation post-commissioning | **20%** | 3 months after commissioning |

*T = date of Sanction Letter issued by MoRTH for funding under the Scheme.*

## Payment Rules

- Initial payment = advance
- Subsequent payments = milestone-based, released after MoRTH PMU review
- State submits implementation / operation report per milestone
- MoRTH PMU reviews before release
- Commissioning deadline: **31 December 2020**
- Payments reconciled against Utilization Certificate (UC)
- National PMU + Dashboard costs retained by MoRTH; state share adjusted proportionately

## Commissioning Deadline

**31 December 2020** — MC must be operational by this date.

## State-Level PMC (Optional)

- State/UT can appoint own PMC or use National PMU agency for state-level PMC services at NICSI rates
- **PMC cost at state level is borne by State/UT** (not funded under Scheme)

## National PMU Structure

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Operator | DIMTS Ltd. |
| Tenure | 3 years (extendable if required) |
| Resources | 1 senior (7+ yrs) + 2 resources (3+ yrs) — full-time at MoRTH |
| Additional | Subject matter experts from DIMTS as needed |
| Fee | **2% of Scheme Value + GST** |
| Fee Schedule | • 1% of Scheme value: half-yearly in advance, equal instalments over 36 months from PMU deployment<br>• 1% of Scheme value: payable alongside milestone-linked fee to states, paid quarterly |

## PMU Activities

- Scrutinize State/UT funding applications
- Monitor Scheme implementation
- Coordinate between MoRTH, States/UTs, MC, NIC, etc.
- Check/verify state implementation + operation reports
- Reconcile payments vs Utilization Certificates
- Resolve AIS 140 / notification compliance queries
- Quarterly scheme review + improvement suggestions
- Periodic (once per milestone) state site visits

## Capacity Building

- States/UTs conduct workshops + training sessions
- Training for Transport Department officials
- Venue: Transport Dept HQ or State/UT-arranged

## Recurring Cost (post initial 2 years — State/UT bears)

- Cloud services
- Connectivity & maintenance for MC
- Helpdesk operations
- AMC for application software + hardware
- Annual map services / data

## Project Note (required in application)

1. Approach for setting up the Monitoring Centre
2. Mechanism currently adopted / interim for CMVR 125H compliance
3. Plans for enforcing CMVR 125H on vehicles registered before 1 Jan 2019
4. Estimated project cost + implementation timeline
