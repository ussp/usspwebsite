## Product Stack

### Core Platform Pillars

1. **Mobile Platform** — on-the-go business operation
2. **Connected Intelligent Platform** — unified data layer
3. **Freight Safety Video Solution**
4. **End-to-End Traceability & Round-Trip Optimization**
5. **Real-Time Visibility & Wagon Tracking**
6. **Cloud-Based Platform & Enterprise Intelligence**
7. **AI-Powered Insights & Video Telematics**
8. **Digital Security & E-Sealing**

### Risk / Exception Management Capabilities

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| In-transit delay | Trip delay alerts, route-wise transit time monitoring, unauthorized stoppage, journey start/end tracking |
| Loading delays | Loading/unloading wait time, idle time at loading, empty vs loaded status, real-time yard presence |
| Breakdowns/accidents | Breakdown alerts, accident detection, sudden stoppage, breakdown-location delay |
| Seal mismatch | Seal tampering detection, open/close monitoring, QR seal mismatch |
| Driver mismatch | Authorized driver verification, driver mismatch ID, over-speeding, driver compliance violations |
| Route deviations | Route deviation, geo-fence violation, red-zone alerts, unauthorized route |

### Uninstallation Checkpoint (Anti-tamper)

1. Verified uninstallation process
2. On-ground physical vehicle audit
3. Immediate plant escalation for anomalies

### AI Mobile App

- Detects vehicles with trip-violation history (e.g. halts in risk zones)
- Proactive monitoring dashboard

### Fleetronix Control Tower

- Trip ETA status, trips onboarded today/yesterday
- Counts: at loading, at unloading, in-transit
- Trips reached/ended
- **Risky trips classification:** power cut, red zone, loading delay, unloading delay, seals mismatch
- Historic violations (vehicles, drivers)
- Exceptions (IGN Off, H-Break)
- Trip status (Loading / Unloading / En-Route / Ended)

### Rail Wagon Visibility (new capability)

- Real-time wagon location across sidings, yards, in-transit
- Arrival/placement/release alerts
- Detention + dwell-time monitoring
- Route & rake consistency tracking
- Exception-based alerts for idle wagons, missed milestones, turnaround optimization

### Freight Safety Video

- Continuous video of trailers, loading bays, doors
- Detect tampering, theft attempts, unsafe handling

### AI Dashcam

- **Video Alarms:** Distraction, unfastened seat belt, yawning, smoking, cell phone use, drowsy driving
- **Alarms:** Fatigue driving, geofence, idle engine, speeding, misuse
- **ADAS:** Tailgating, Forward Collision Warning, Traffic Speed Violated
- **GPS-Based Alarms**
- **Live Video Streaming** — multi-channel per vehicle

### Fuel Sensor Solution

- Real-time fuel levels (high accuracy)
- Detect fuel theft, sudden drops, unauthorized usage
- Efficiency + cost control

1. **Infra-first, not app-first** — foundational data/visibility/control rails
2. **Modular, not monolithic** — each persona gets just what they need
3. **Self-evolving flywheel** — every trip feeds intelligence
4. **Capex-light, ops-free, scalable** — product-led, no fleet/warehouse/escrow
5. **India-specific behavioral DNA** — voice-first, panic routing, unbundled pricing "Bharat in code"
