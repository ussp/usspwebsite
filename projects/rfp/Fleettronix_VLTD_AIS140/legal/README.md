## Critical Legal Risks to Flag to Counsel

1. **Section 27 Indian Contract Act** — general non-compete covenants are void in India. The NCA relies on a narrow, legitimate-interest defence. Counsel must confirm enforceability based on latest case law (e.g., *Niranjan Shankar Golikari*, *Gujarat Bottling v Coca Cola*, *Percept D'Mark*).
2. **Stamping** — all three documents require stamping under Telangana Stamp Act. Amounts and procedures to be confirmed.
3. **Authority to sign** — confirm board resolutions are in place for MD (USSPPL) and Co-Founder (Fleetronix).
4. **Entity name verification** — Fleetronix's full registered legal entity name and CIN must be confirmed before drafting.
5. **Liquidated damages reasonableness** — 3x multiplier may be challenged as a penalty; counsel to assess.
6. **Pre-existing carve-outs** — both Parties must exchange a written schedule of pre-existing engagements (Fleetronix's prior AP Govt deck work in particular).
