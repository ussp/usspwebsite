## 5. Sensor-based / IoT solutions for US businesses

USSP designs, deploys, and operates sensor-driven platforms for US enterprise customers — the same architecture pattern that an AIS 140 Monitoring Centre is built on.

### Capability stack

#### Edge — devices

- Vehicle telematics units (GPS, IMU, CAN-bus).
- Industrial sensors (temperature, vibration, pressure, flow, energy).
- Panic / SOS button and dashcam variants.
- Cellular and LPWAN connectivity.

#### Pipeline — ingest

- MQTT / HTTPS device-to-cloud ingestion.
- Edge buffering for low-coverage areas.
- Event normalization and validation.
- Geo-fence and rule engine in stream.

#### Platform — analyze

- Time-series data lake on AWS / Azure.
- Real-time alerting and incident workflow.
- AI / ML for anomaly detection and predictive maintenance.
- Dashboards and APIs for downstream systems.

#### Operate — sustain

- 24×7 NOC + SOC monitoring.
- Device-fleet lifecycle management and OTA updates.
- SLA-backed managed-services contracts.
- Compliance: SOC 2, HIPAA where applicable.

### Where this shows up in our US delivery

| Domain | Use cases |
|---|---|
| Fleet & logistics | GPS + driver-behavior telemetry, route compliance, after-hours alerting |
| Industrial / facilities | Sensor networks for predictive maintenance, energy, asset utilization |
| Healthcare operations | Connected-device data flowing into compliant clinical and ops dashboards |
| Safety & compliance | Panic, geofence, and incident-response workflows for distributed workforces |
