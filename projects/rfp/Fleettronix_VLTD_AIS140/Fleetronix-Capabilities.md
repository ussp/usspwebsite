## 3. What Fleetronix does

Fleetronix is an **enterprise fleet administration platform** built on IoT and cloud, with three operating principles: visibility, security, efficiency. The platform unifies vehicle tracking, cargo integrity, driver-safety analytics, and rule-based alerting into a single control tower for fleet operators.

### Mission — "Swift · Smart · Intelligent"

- **Swift:** fast, responsive, and timely fleet operations at every step.
- **Smart:** data-driven decisions for optimized and efficient performance.
- **Intelligent:** advanced technology that delivers accurate insights and proactive solutions.

### Vision — "FLEET"

- **F**uture Ready — future-ready fleet and mobility solutions.
- **L**eadership — leading innovation in fleet technology.
- **E**fficiency — enhancing operational efficiency.
- **E**xcellence — excellence in service and delivery.
- **T**echnology — technology-led transformation.

### Positioning (in their own words)

- **What does Fleetronix do?** Provides in-transit cargo theft prevention technology and services.
- **Why do customers choose Fleetronix?** 24×7 support, end-to-end service, secure and reliable tracking at low cost.
- **Customer benefits:** reduced losses, improved compliance, full operational visibility.

## 4. Solution stack

### Mobile platform + connected intelligent platform

- Easy-to-adopt user-friendly mobile platform for on-the-go operations.
- Cloud-based platform with enterprise intelligence.
- Real-time visibility & wagon tracking.
- Freight safety video solution.
- End-to-end traceability & round-trip optimization.
- AI-powered insights & video telematics.
- Digital security & e-sealing.

### Fleetronix Control Tower

A unified dashboard giving operators:

- Trips ETA status.
- Trips onboarded — yesterday and today.
- Count of vehicles at loading / unloading points.
- Count of vehicles in transit.
- Trips reached and ended.
- Risky trips: power cut, red zone, load tampering.

### AI-driven mobile application

- Detects and surfaces vehicles with a history of trip violations.
- Flags halts in designated risk zones.
- Enables proactive monitoring and improved operational safety.

### Differentiators

- Visibility is the signature advantage — not just a feature.
- QR-based cargo sealing and validation systems for tamper-proof logistics.
- NPR (Number Plate Recognition) camera integrations for automated vehicle identification.
- Wagon-tracking solutions for rail logistics visibility.
- Video telematics + AI driver monitoring.
- Risky-zone and red-zone mapping with intelligent alerts.
- AI anomaly and unauthorized-activity detection.
- On-ground operational advisory and execution support.
- Highly accurate IoT-based fleet monitoring.

### Field operations differentiators

- "From source to destination, we manage exceptions."
- "Not just tracking trucks — we track risk, responsibility & results."
- Verified uninstallation checks at trip-end.
- On-ground physical vehicle audit.
- Immediate plant escalation for anomalies.

## 5. Newer capabilities

### Rail wagon visibility and control

- Real-time wagon location across sidings, yards, and in-transit rail sections.
- Arrival, placement, and release alerts at source, intermediate, and destination sidings.
- Detention and dwell-time monitoring to identify loading/unloading and yard delays.

### Freight safety video solution

- Continuous video monitoring of trailers, loading bays, and doors.
- Detects tampering, theft attempts, and unsafe handling of freight.
- "Protect every load, not just every trip."

### Live video streaming

- For vehicles with an active camera, the user can watch a live stream of the desired camera channel.

### AI dashcam alarms

| Category | Alarms |
|---|---|
| Video alarms | Distraction · unfastened seat belt · yawning · smoking · cell-phone usage · drowsy driving |
| GPS-based alarms | Fatigue driving · geofence · idle engine · speeding · misuse |
| ADAS alarms | Tailgating · forward collision warning · traffic-speed violation |

### Fuel sensor solution

- Monitors real-time fuel levels with high accuracy.
- Detects fuel theft, sudden drops, and unauthorized usage.
- Helps improve efficiency and control fuel costs.

## 6. Market evolution — Fleetronix 1.0 → 2.0 → X

| Stage | Description |
|---|---|
| Fleetronix 1.0 | Single-purpose tracking utility — current competitive baseline. |
| Fleetronix 2.0 | "From utility to marketplace" — shipper search engine and auto-matchmaking; smart shipper discovery for transporters, categorized and verified. |
| Fleetronix X | "From marketplace to full ecosystem" — load sharing & return matching, gamification, ecosystem-level platform extending from drivers to shippers. |

### Why Fleetronix wins (their stated thesis)

- **Infra-first, not app-first** — built foundational rails (data, visibility, control), let the flow emerge.
- **Modular, not monolithic** — drivers, transporters, and shippers don't need one big app, they need just-right tools.
- "Not a logistics app. A logistics OS."
