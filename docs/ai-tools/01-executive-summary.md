## What Is the AI Transformation Monitor?

The AI Transformation Monitor is a web-based tool that **measures the impact of AI training on Scrum team productivity**. USSP consultants use it to:

1. **Measure baseline** — Capture how a team performs before AI training
2. **Deliver training** — Customized by role (developer, QA, Scrum Master, PO, DevOps)
3. **Measure again** — Capture performance after training + ramp-up period
4. **Show improvement** — Generate research-backed comparison reports

## Why This Tool Exists

Organizations spend significant budgets on AI training but have no standardized way to prove it worked. This tool fills that gap with **quantitative, auditable, research-backed evidence**.

## Our Philosophy: Amplify, Not Replace

Unlike companies using AI to replace workers (as seen with Oracle's mass layoffs in March 2026), USSP's approach is fundamentally different:

> **The same team delivers more. No one loses their job. Everyone becomes more effective.**

This tool proves that thesis with data.

## Research Foundation

The tool measures across three peer-reviewed frameworks:

- **DORA Metrics** (Google/Accelerate) — Deployment frequency, lead time, failure rate, recovery time
- **SPACE Framework** (Microsoft Research) — Satisfaction, Performance, Activity, Communication, Efficiency
- **DevEx Framework** (ACM Queue) — Flow state, feedback loops, cognitive load
