## Signing In

1. Navigate to **https://tools.ussp.co** (or `http://localhost:3002` for local dev)
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Use your authorized USSP Google account

**Can't sign in?** Your email must be in the `staff_users` table with an active account. Contact an admin (vinay@, swapan@, or arjun@ lagisetty.com) to add you.

## Understanding the Dashboard

After signing in, you'll see the Dashboard with:

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Active Engagements** | Engagements currently in baseline, training, or post-assessment phase |
| **Teams Assessed** | Total number of teams across all engagements |
| **Avg Improvement** | Average improvement percentage across completed engagements |
| **Assessments In Progress** | Assessments that haven't been finalized yet |

Below the cards, you'll see a table of **Recent Engagements** with links to each one.

## Navigation

The sidebar has three main sections:

| Menu Item | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of all engagements and key metrics |
| **Engagements** | List, create, and manage client engagements |
| **Methodology** | Research citations and framework explanations |

## The Complete Workflow

Here's the end-to-end process:

```
Step 1: Create Engagement     → Name the project, select client, choose integration
Step 2: Add Teams              → Add one or more Scrum teams with their members
Step 3: Baseline Assessment    → Measure 3-6 sprints of pre-training performance
Step 4: Generate Training Plan → AI recommends tools and training per role
Step 5: Deliver Training       → (Outside the tool) Conduct the AI training
Step 6: Post-Training Assessment → Measure 3-6 sprints of post-training performance
Step 7: View Report            → Compare before vs after with research benchmarks
Step 8: Share Results           → Export for client stakeholders
```

## Roles & Permissions

| Role | What You Can Do |
|------|----------------|
| **Admin / Superadmin** | Full access — create, edit, delete engagements, assessments, training plans, view reports |
| **Recruiter** | Read-only access to engagements, assessments, and reports |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to everything |
