## Step 2: Register with the State Board of Elections (BOE)

**Required if:** Aggregate bids/contracts exceed $50,000 annually across all Illinois state agencies. Since USSP is a TOPS subvendor, this applies to us.

**Important:** You must register BEFORE submitting any bids. There is no grace period.

### Steps:
1. Go to **https://berep.elections.il.gov**
2. Create a new registration for **US Software Professionals Inc.**
3. Provide business entity information and ownership details
4. Complete and submit the registration
5. Save your **BOE Registration Number** — you will need this for IPG

### Compliance Note:
Illinois Procurement Code (Section 50-37) restricts political contributions to gubernatorial candidates and affiliated entities. Violations can void contracts. Be aware of contribution limits during active state contracts.

**Contact:**
- Springfield: (217) 782-4141
- Chicago: (312) 814-6440

## Step 3: Obtain IDHR Bidder Eligibility Number

**Required if:** Your business currently employs or has employed 15+ persons in the past 365 days.

### Steps:
1. Complete the **"Employer Report Form (PC-1)"**
2. Submit to the Illinois Department of Human Rights (IDHR) Public Contracts Unit
3. Pay the **$75 non-refundable fee**
4. Wait for processing — the IDHR number is **valid for 5 years** once issued

### Where to Submit:

Chicago, IL 60661

- Phone: (312) 814-6200

You still need to file the PC-1 form, but you may receive an exemption number instead.

## Step 5: Complete IPG Vendor Registration

After creating your account:

1. **Log in** at https://ipg.illinois.gov
2. Go to your **Dashboard**
3. Under **"Key Actions"** on the right side, click **"Start/Renew Vendor Registration"**
4. Click the gray button **"New Vendor Registration"**
5. From the list, select **"State of Illinois Vendor Registration"**
6. Choose **"Autofill"** if available (pre-populates from your account info), or start fresh

### Complete All Required Sections:

The registration has multiple forms/sections. Fill out each carefully:

| Section | What's Needed |
|---------|---------------|
| **Business Info** | Legal name, DBA, entity type, address (pre-filled from Step 4) |
| **Ownership** | Names and ownership percentages of all owners |
| **Financial Info** | Company financial details as requested |
| **IDHR** | Enter your IDHR Public Contract Number from Step 3 |
| **Board of Elections** | Select "Yes, I certify my business is registered with BOE" and enter your BOE Registration Number from Step 2 |
| **Certifications** | Check any applicable: minority-owned, veteran-owned, small business, etc. |
| **Attachments** | Upload any required supporting documents |

7. **Review** all sections for completeness and accuracy
8. **Submit** for review

### What Happens Next:
- A CPO (Chief Procurement Office) staff member reviews your application
- **Most first-time applications are returned for revision** — this is normal
- You'll receive an email if anything needs correction
- Fix any issues and resubmit
- Once approved, you'll receive your **IPG Registration Number** via email

**Estimated timeline:** 1-2 weeks for approval (longer if revisions needed)

---

## Step 6: Access BidBuy

Once your IPG registration is approved, a BidBuy account is **automatically created** for you.

1. Go to **https://www.bidbuy.illinois.gov/bso/**
2. Click **"Login Assistance"**
3. Follow the prompts to activate your BidBuy account using your IPG credentials
4. Once logged in, search for:
   - **BidBuy # P-59947** (TOPS contract)
   - **Contract # CMT4599470**
5. Download the contract documents, including the **rate card**
